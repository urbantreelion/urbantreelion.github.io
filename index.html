Work

TransLink - Coast Mountain Bus Company

Electrical Engineering Student Assistant• May 2018 - Aug 2018

Member of the Fleet Maintenance Engineering team working on both preventative and active maintenance on TransLink’s 1500+ buses. This included commissioning of the new SR2210 CNG buses, inspections on hybrid buses and conventional diesel buses, trouble shooting on all types of buses, and much more. I gained familiarity into the Can-Bus network protocols, wireless network connectivity, and common electrical issues onboard the buses.

Fisheries and Oceans Canada - Canadian Coast Guard

Electrical Engineering Co-op Student• Sept 2017 - Apr 2018

As a co-op student with the DFO / CCG I witnessed firsthand the difficulties faced when designing and building a multi million-dollar vessel and the solutions used to overcome these challenges. I was tasked with completing electrical and mechanical inspections onboard the first two vessels, and to provide input for possible to solutions to any problems. Accountability and confidentiality were key characteristics of the role because the work often involved handling sensitive information.

The Strongman Group

Solar Energy Analyst• Jan 2017 - Apr 2017

My role with TSG was to help answer the question: can we incorporate solar energy into our existing properties? To answer this, I drafted preliminary roof-mounted solar energy systems for various TSG properties. These drafts included system design techniques for sizing and installing photovoltaic solar systems as well as a method to analyze the value of a proposed system to ensure financial feasibility of the system before purchase of materials. My work with TSG was nominated for the University of Victoria’s Engineering Associates Work Term Report Excellence Award. This award recognizes the top engineering co-op reports each semester at UVic.

BC Ferries

Terminal Attendant• Summer 2014 - 2016

I worked at BC Ferries in Horseshoe Bay for three consecutive summers. My key responsibilities were safely directing traffic, loading and unloading vehicles on and off the vessels, ensure pedestrians safely move thorughout the terminal, answer customer inquires, perform security checks, and sanitary duties.
